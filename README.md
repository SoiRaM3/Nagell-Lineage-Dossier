## Summary Overview

- The Nagell family is attested as **armigerous in Norway by 1445**
- The family bore **canting arms featuring three nails**
- The lineage operated within the medieval and early modern Scandinavian social order
- Members later appear in clerical and administrative roles
- Documentary evidence indicates cross-Baltic connections in the early modern period
- Based on currently available documentation, the **Nagell surname line appears to
  terminate with the Stokknes family as the last known name-bearers**

This statement reflects documentation only and does not imply succession,
custodianship, or inheritance beyond surname continuity.
