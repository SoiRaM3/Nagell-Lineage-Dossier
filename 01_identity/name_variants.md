# Nagell – Name Variants and Orthography

This document lists known and plausible **orthographic variants** of the
surname **Nagell** as they appear in historical sources.

Variant spellings are common in medieval and early modern records and should
not be interpreted as distinct lineages unless supported by contextual evidence.

---

## General Notes on Orthographic Variation

Before modern spelling conventions, names were typically written:
- phonetically
- according to local dialect
- according to the scribe’s linguistic background
- without concern for standardization

As a result, variation in spelling is expected and does not imply discontinuity.

---

## Recorded and Plausible Variants

The following variants are attested or contextually plausible within
Scandinavian and related Germanic documentary traditions:

- **Nagell**

- **Nägel** (Germanic-influenced spelling)

## Linguistic Context

The name is linguistically associated with the Germanic word for **nail**
(Norwegian *nagel*, German *Nagel*), aligning with the canting nature of the
Nagell coat of arms.

This linguistic alignment supports continuity between name, symbolism, and
heraldic usage.
