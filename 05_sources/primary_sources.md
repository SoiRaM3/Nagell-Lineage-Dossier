# Primary Sources

This document lists **categories of primary sources** relevant to the Nagell
family.

Specific references are included where known; absence does not imply non-
existence.

---

## Categories of Primary Sources

Primary material relevant to the Nagell lineage may include:

- parish registers
- ecclesiastical records
- administrative and civic documents
- seal impressions
- armorial manuscripts
- legal or notarial records

---

## Characteristics

Primary sources are treated as authoritative where:
- contemporaneous with the events described
- internally consistent
- not contradicted by stronger evidence

Interpretation remains limited to what the source supports.

---

## Documentation Limits

Not all primary material survives, and not all surviving material is accessible.

This list reflects known source categories rather than an exhaustive catalogue.
