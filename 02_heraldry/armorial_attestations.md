## Early Attestation

The Nagell family is attested as armigerous in Norway by **1445**, placing the
coat of arms within the medieval heraldic tradition of Scandinavia.

This attestation indicates recognized use of heraldic symbols at a time when
such usage was restricted to established armigerous lineages.

This consistency supports the interpretation of deliberate heraldic usage.
